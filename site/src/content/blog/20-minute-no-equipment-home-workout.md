You don't need a gym or equipment to get a great workout. This 20-minute routine uses only your bodyweight and can be done in your living room.

**Warm-Up (2 minutes)**

Start with arm circles and leg swings to prepare your muscles. Do 30 seconds of marching in place to elevate your heart rate.

**Main Workout (16 minutes)**

Perform each exercise for 45 seconds with 15 seconds rest: push-ups, squats, lunges, mountain climbers, burpees, plank hold. Repeat this circuit 2-3 times.

**Cool Down (2 minutes)**

End with stretching. Focus on your legs, shoulders, and back to prevent soreness and improve flexibility.

**Making It Your Own**

Modify intensity by adjusting speed or reps. Rest longer if needed. The goal is consistency, not perfection. Do this 3-4 times weekly for best results.
